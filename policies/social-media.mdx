We have established specific policies regarding the presentation of your work on social media and online platforms.

<Info>
  You are expected to adhere to these policies when referencing Verita, its clients, or any work completed through our platform.
</Info>

## On LinkedIn and Professional Networks

### What you can do

- Indicate that you served as a contractor for **Verita Artificial Intelligence**
- Share high-level details regarding the nature of the project you participated in

<Tip>
  **Example:** "Developed training data for large language models by formulating coding problems that models could not resolve, and subsequently documenting the correct solutions."
</Tip>

### What you should NOT do

- List a client as your employer
- Indicate or suggest that you were employed full-time by Verita
- Use "Verita" instead of "Verita Artificial Intelligence" to specify the organization
- Fabricate or inflate job titles
- Share screenshots, detailed examples, or confidential material

## On the Internet

### Allowed

- Share your experiences with the Verita platform or AI interviews
- Discuss your onboarding process at Verita or your project management activities
- Provide high-level summaries of the work you have undertaken
- Consider how skills acquired through Verita projects can be applied in other areas of your life

### Strictly Prohibited

<Warning>
  The following activities are not permitted:

  - Posting screenshots or content from completed tasks
  - Sharing questions asked by others in your project
  - Discussing clients by name or any client-specific work
  - Reusing work produced for Verita or its clients elsewhere
</Warning>

## When in doubt

In cases of uncertainty, maintain a high-level overview and refrain from providing specifics.

If there is any doubt regarding the appropriateness of sharing certain information, please contact [info@verita-ai.com](mailto:info@verita-ai.com) prior to making a posting.
